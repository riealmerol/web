Welcome to Marie’s Handmade Shop

Marie’s Shop is your destination for delightful handmade items, made with love and passion. From vibrant mugs to comfy t-shirts and sandals perfect for summer, our products are crafted to bring a smile to your face and warmth to your space.

Based in the heart of Roxas, Marie’s Handmade Shop is more than just a store — it’s a celebration of creativity, dedication, and community. Every item in our shop is carefully designed and handcrafted by local artisans who put their hearts into every detail. We believe in supporting local talent and bringing their beautiful work into your homes.

Whether you’re looking for a thoughtful gift or something to brighten up your everyday life, you’ll find it here. Our collection includes handmade mugs that are perfect for your morning coffee, soft and stylish t-shirts for any occasion, and sandals that are both comfortable and fashionable for sunny days. We also offer curated gift bundles that are ideal for birthdays, holidays, or simply letting someone know you care.

Customization is at the heart of what we do. We understand that everyone is unique, which is why we offer personalized designs and made-to-order items. If you have a special request, our team is more than happy to bring your vision to life. We take pride in ensuring each piece reflects your style and personality.

At Marie’s Handmade Shop, we are driven by a simple mission: to bring you joy through artful, crafted goods that stand the test of time. Our values are rooted in sustainability, quality, and authenticity. We use eco-friendly materials wherever possible and strive to reduce waste in our packaging and production processes. When you shop with us, you are not only getting a beautiful product — you are making a positive impact.

Thank you for supporting our dream and being a part of our growing community. We are excited to continue creating and sharing handmade happiness with you. Visit often, discover something new, and let us help you celebrate life’s special moments — one handcrafted piece at a time.
